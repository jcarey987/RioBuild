At Your Service

Design-Assist

We provide constructability, financial, and sequential feedback throughout the design phase. The result is less RFIs and change orders.

Estimating

Estimating is an important part of the construction process. Costs of physical construction are forecasted to aid in making decisions.

Scheduling

Our schedules are arranged using the Critical Path Method. The schedule helps optimize opportunites through efficient sequencing of the construction process.

Quality Control

Quality management that is process and product focused. Before the project, we determine how likely a contractor will meet project needs, and during we verify the product meets expectations.

Owner Representation

Owner representation that is client focused. We can offer assistance in selecting a design group, general contractor, and procurement methods. As an owner agent your involvement in construction can be as much or as little as your business demands allow.

Project Management

Big project? We can help contractors with cost, schedule, deliveries, and other project management needs as contract employees.
